At 52 years old, I am a guy who really enjoys growing peppers. I live and work in Rancho Bernardo, a community in San Diego, CA. Living in San Diego, there are plenty of sunny days to grow super hot pepper. I grew up in Omaha, Nebraska, and did not like peppers until I moved to San Diego. The hottest pepper in the world is a Smokin' Ed's Carolina Reaper, shown in the above photo.
